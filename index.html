About Our Symposium

Welcome to our symposium on Natural Language Processing (NLP) in Biology and Chemistry! Join leading researchers and practitioners in Switzerland as we explore the transformative potential of NLP in unlocking insights from vast biological and chemical datasets.

Discover cutting-edge applications, foster collaborations, and shape the future of NLP in these exciting fields. Don't miss this opportunity to be part of the scientific community driving innovation at the intersection of language and science.
